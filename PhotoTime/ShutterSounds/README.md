Bundled shutter-sound assets live in this directory.

These files are shipped as short "style" cues rather than strict one-to-one
device reproductions. Some are trimmed from openly licensed source recordings,
and some remain synthesized placeholders until better sources are available.

Filenames that the app loads:

- canon-eos-style.m4a
- nikon-dslr-style.m4a
- sony-alpha-style.m4a
- panasonic-lumix-style.m4a
- fujifilm-x-style.m4a
- hasselblad-500cm-style.m4a
- leica-m-style.m4a

Current mapping:

- `canon-eos-style.m4a`: trimmed from a real Canon T50 shutter recording on Wikimedia Commons
- `nikon-dslr-style.m4a`: trimmed from a Nikon D850 shutter recording sourced from Pixabay
- `sony-alpha-style.m4a`: trimmed from a generic SLR burst recording on Wikimedia Commons
- `panasonic-lumix-style.m4a`: trimmed from the same CC0 digital SLR burst source, tuned as a Lumix-style near-silent digital cue
- `fujifilm-x-style.m4a`: trimmed from a short digital camera / phone shutter recording on Wikimedia Commons
- `hasselblad-500cm-style.m4a`: mechanical medium-format style cue, currently an approximation pending a better Hasselblad-specific open recording
- `leica-m-style.m4a`: trimmed from a Holga mechanical shutter recording on Wikimedia Commons

See `ATTRIBUTION.md` in the same folder for the exact source URLs and license notes.
