Shutter sound source notes

The app ships short edited cues derived from the following public source files.
Edits include trimming, fades, gain adjustment, and AAC transcoding for app use.

Canon EOS style
- Output file: `canon-eos-style.m4a`
- Source page: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Canon_T50_shutter_noise.ogg
- Direct source file: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/88/Canon_T50_shutter_noise.ogg
- License: CC BY-SA 3.0
- Notes: real Canon T50 shutter recording, used as a "Canon EOS style" cue

Nikon DSLR style
